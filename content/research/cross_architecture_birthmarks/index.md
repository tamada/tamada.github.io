## 🗣️ Overview

This research topic proposes a cross-architecture software birthmarks that targets the real-world executables such as Linux ELF, Windows PE, and macOS Mach-O.
The birthmarks are extracted from the intermediate representation of the executables throughGhidra's p-code.
